Tournament Rules

Tournament Format

  • Bracket System: Single elimination tournament with finale
  • Quarter Finals: 3 matches between 6 teams (1 team gets bye to semi)
  • Semi Finals: 2 matches between 4 teams
  • Championship Final: Winner takes all finale match

Scoring System

  • Elimination: Winner advances, loser is eliminated
  • No ties: Each match must have a clear winner
  • Advancement: Win to move to next round
  • Final: Winner of championship final is tournament champion

Awards

  • 1st Place: Tournament Champions
  • 2nd Place: Runners-up
  • 3rd Place: Bronze medalists
